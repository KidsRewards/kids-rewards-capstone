KidsRewards is an application that can allow parents and teachers to assign tasks and provide associated rewards. The objective of the application is to establish a foundational sense of financial responsibility and goal achievement between parents and their children. Assign your kids/students a task and decide how many points each task is worth. Once the child finishes the task and marks it complete then you can approve it. The child can keep track of there accumulated points and make a wishlist of items they would like to purchase with their points. If you approve the wishlist item you can also assign how many points is needed to buy that item. The item can now be tracked in the rewards store til you purchase it.
